Doors Cafe

Located behind Byblos Bank in Al-Shaalan, Doors Café is a small and cozy place in the heart of Damascus.
It opens every day from morning until midnight, offering coffee, soft drinks, and light meals.
The café has free Wi-Fi, making it a good spot to relax, meet friends, or work on your laptop.
With its calm atmosphere and friendly service, Doors Café is a nice place to enjoy simple moments in comfort.
